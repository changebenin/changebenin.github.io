CHANGE works where health, environment, and equity intersect-helping communities anticipate risks, reduce harm, and build resilience in Benin and beyond.

About CHANGE

CHANGE is a non-political, non-profit research center that transforms scientific knowledge into concrete, sustainable actions. We respond to climate-sensitive health risks-emerging infectious diseases, antimicrobial resistance, and environmental pollutants-through evidence-based, locally adapted solutions. Our work advances the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, especially SDG 3 (Good Health & Well-being), SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation) and SDG 13 (Climate Action).

Focus Areas

AMR & Emerging Diseases

Detect & Respond

Advanced genomics and transmission-network insights to track pathogens and support targeted responses, including bacteriophages and credible antibiotic alternatives.

Environmental Pollutants

Reduce Exposure

Monitor and mitigate CECs-microplastics, pharmaceutical residues, pesticides, persistent chemicals-to protect health and ecosystems.

Capacity & Early Warning

Build Systems

Training, early-warning systems, and preventive strategies co-designed with local actors for rapid, context-appropriate action.

Gender & Inclusion

Genter Equity

Embed gender equity and social inclusion, ensuring active participation of women, children, and marginalized groups across all activities.

Climate Resilience

Adapt & Thrive

Support communities to assess risks and adopt sustainable solutions to climate impacts, strengthening resilience over time.
