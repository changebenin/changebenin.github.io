About CHANGE

CHANGE is a non-political, non-profit research center that transforms scientific knowledge into concrete, sustainable actions. We respond to climate-sensitive health risks—emerging infectious diseases, antimicrobial resistance, and environmental pollutants—through evidence-based, locally adapted solutions. Our work advances the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, especially SDG 3 (Good Health & Well-being), SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation) and SDG 13 (Climate Action).

Our Vision

A world where every community—especially those most vulnerable to climate shocks—can anticipate health risks early and act decisively to protect lives, dignity, and ecosystems.

Our Mission

To co-create practical, science-based solutions that reduce climate-sensitive health risks. We blend advanced genomics, environmental monitoring, and social inclusion to build equitable early-warning and response systems.

Strategic Objectives

1) Anticipate

Develop no/low-tech early warning tools with communities to detect climate–health signals in real time.

2) Reduce Risk

Map transmission pathways, pollutants, and AMR drivers to inform prevention, stewardship, and targeted interventions.

3) Build Capacity

Train local actors and strengthen institutions for sustained monitoring, rapid response, and data stewardship.

4) Innovate

Translate research into scalable tools—dashboards, protocols, and policy briefs—for decision-makers and practitioners.

5) Center Equity

Embed gender and inclusion so women, children, and marginalized groups shape priorities and benefit from outcomes.

6) Share & Inspire

Communicate evidence clearly to mobilize action across government, academia, civil society, and communities.

Focus Areas

AMR & Emerging Diseases

Detect & Respond

Advanced genomics and transmission-network insights to track pathogens and support targeted responses, including bacteriophages and credible antibiotic alternatives.

Environmental Pollutants

Reduce Exposure

Monitor and mitigate CECs—microplastics, pharmaceutical residues, pesticides, persistent chemicals—to protect health and ecosystems.

Capacity & Early Warning

Build Systems

Training, early-warning systems, and preventive strategies co-designed with local actors for rapid, context-appropriate action.

Gender & Inclusion

Gender Equity

Embed gender equity and social inclusion, ensuring active participation of women, children, and marginalized groups across all activities.

Climate Resilience

Adapt & Thrive

Support communities to assess risks and adopt sustainable solutions to climate impacts, strengthening resilience over time.

From Science to Practice

Translate & Share

Turn research into practical tools for communities, decision-makers, and partners; communicate science clearly for real-world impact.
